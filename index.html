We Are Providing A Delicious Food

Classico Italian Cafe,one of the best pizza restaurants in Bholaram , Indore, India, provides delectable dishes imbued with the chef's trademark charm. For those looking for authentic flavors when indulging in a meal, Classico Italian Cafe is the closest you can get in Indore to an authentic Italian pizza experience without traveling to Italy. Every dish, including the toppings on your pizza, is made from scratch using garden-fresh vegetables and herbs. Classico Italiano Cafe's classic Margherita Pizza, RP Special Pizza, Thin Crust Pizza, and Puff Pizza is our favorite dish to order.
